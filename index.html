Royal is now recruiting!

Royal is a new raiding guild with a Thursday/Sunday 8:30PM - 11:30PM schedule. We are a semi-hardcore guild, looking to complete Heroic Hellfire Citadel and gather exceptional raiders for Mythic content. Please visit the About page for more information, and apply if you feel you are a good fit for our guild!
Posted on July 16, 2015

Progression: H HFC: 8/13, N HFC: 12/13

Recognizing the need to clear Normal Hellfire Citadel for class trinkets and tier pieces, we have decided to dedicate Thursday and Sunday of this week to finishing Normal difficulty. That being said, we also have been organizing impromptu, optional raids on Tuesdays, 8:30PM, with loot handled by regular rolls. Normally, these Tuesday raids would be used to clear Normal content in one raid day, but given the decision to clear Normal on our regular raid days, we instead progressed on Heroic on Tuesday.

Although we did not have the full guild roster on Tuesday, we were able to down (H) Socrethar and (H) Iskar, bringing our guild progression to 8/13 on Heroic.

On Thursday, we defeated (N) Tyrant Velhari and (N) Mannoroth despite facing severe lag issues that affected both of our tanks.

The next scheduled raid on Sunday, July 26, will be focused on (N) Archimonde. For all scheduled raids afterwards, we will be reverting to our original plan of Heroic progression on Thursdays and Sundays, with possible, optional, Normal runs on Tuesdays.

Important Note about Tuesdays: Please be aware that these Tuesday runs are subject to be cancelled at any time, and are not in any way planned beforehand. Thus, it will not count towards EPGP, and since we are expecting a non-significant number of pugs that will be required (due to schedule constraints from various raid members), loot will be MS>OS rolls.
